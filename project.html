Railway Signal Detector

NIMBUS 2025 LoRa ESP32

An innovative system enabling trains to receive signal information 2km in advance during foggy conditions, enhancing safety and preventing accidents.

Challenge

During foggy conditions, train operators often struggle to see railway signals in time, increasing accident risks. Traditional visual signals become ineffective in poor visibility.

Solution

Implemented LoRa communication technology with ESP microcontrollers to create a reliable wireless communication system that's effective even in adverse weather conditions.

Key Features

  • Long-range signal transmission (up to 2km) in adverse weather
  • Directional antennas for accurate signal targeting
  • Low-power consumption for sustainable operation
  • Real-time signal status updates to train operators
  • Weather-resistant hardware design

Team Member - Responsible for LoRa communication protocol implementation and antenna design
